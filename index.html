What is ionOS ?

Ion Os is a flashable Custom ROM to bring a true Pixel feel to your Android Device at first glance, with many additional configurations at your disposal. We aim to provide frequent builds with monthly security patches from the latest aosp sources.

Features

Ion is an AOSP based ROM with Pixel feel and some useful customizations.Focused on Stability along with features.

Fast

We managed to keep the OS as light as possible without removing any feature to give a lagfree experience.

Open Source

Our firmware sources are Completely Open Source and Being Maintained on Github Organisation.

Up to Date

We Provide OTA updates with Monthly security patches besides Extras Features.Our Officially Supported devices will get Notified with Latest releases and updates.
